My Learning Contract

GAIN

  • I expect this subject to be able to cover every topic in the course since it is a fundamental step in my would-be IT career.
  • I expect that it would not be a passive subject.
  • I expect that it would involve activities that would be useful in real-life applications.
  • I expect that it would be challenging, but not too tedious.
  • I expect that it would not only develop my digital skills, but also my work ethic.

GIVE

  • To be able to pass this subject, I would make sure to submit every assignment on time.
  • I would make use of my time wisely in lab activities.
  • I would be willing to give financial contributions to group works if necessary.
  • I would listen carefully to insructions and lectures.
  • I would try my best to get to class on time.

MOTIVATE

  • I need to graduate early to help my parents put my younger siblings to school.
  • I need to make sure that I wouldn't be a failure because it would be shameful.
  • I want to be rich.
  • I want to be an active contributor to the well-being of our society.
  • If I graduate, with flying colors hopefully, it would bring the biggest pride to myself.

PREVENT

  • To make sure that I won't fail, I wouldn't be sloppy and lazy in every activity.
  • I wouldn't be tempted to slack off or cut classes.
  • I would not procrastinate.
  • I would not give in to school pressure.
  • I would not be too serious in this subject because that would lead to a fast burnout.
